The Route

Greece: Samos to Thessaloniki by sea. Riding through Egnatia Odos to Igoumenitsa port.

Italy: Ferry to Ancona (Air Seat). Riding to Bologna to stay with Evanthia. Exploring the medieval porticos and the historic center.

France: Entry via Menton. Visit to the medieval village of Saint-Guilhem-le-Désert before arriving at Montolieu.
